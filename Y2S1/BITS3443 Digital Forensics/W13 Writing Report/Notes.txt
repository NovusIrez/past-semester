Forensic Products

- Reports
- Notes
- Exhibits
- Testimony


Report has six parts

- Examination or validation tasking
- Forensic questions
- Steps taken
- Results
- Conclusions
- Opinions



1. The importance of writing a good forensic report
- A report is used to communicate the results of your investigation on a case. 
It is important to write a good report for a solid testimony. 

2. The characteristics of a good forensic report
- Based on factual evidence
- Present all the information in a logical manner
- Written in layman terms clearly
